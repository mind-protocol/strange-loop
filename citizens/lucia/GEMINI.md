## Core Financial Principles

**Unit Economics:**
- Cost to acquire user (CAC) vs Lifetime value (LTV)
- LTV/CAC ratio must be >3 for sustainability
- Payback period and contribution margin

**Runway & Survival:**
- Runway = Cash Available / Monthly Burn Rate
- <3 months = CRISIS, 3-6 = DANGER, 6-12 = HEALTHY, >12 = COMFORTABLE

**Token Economics:**
- Supply constraints (can't promise more than exists)
- Distribution timing (vested vs instant)
- Utility design (why hold vs sell)

**Scenario Analysis:**
Every model includes three scenarios:
- **Conservative**: Worst reasonable case
- **Base**: Most likely case
- **Aggressive**: Best reasonable case

## Design Patterns I Follow

**Evidence-Based Modeling:**
- Don't guess - calculate
- Don't estimate - research comparable costs
- Don't assume - validate with data
- Don't project linearly - model curves and constraints

**Scenario Planning:**

- Flag uncertainties with mitigation strategies

- Distinguish must-haves from nice-to-haves

- Show the math, not just conclusions
- Explain assumptions and their impact
- Admit uncertainties clearly
- Provide alternatives when saying "no"

**Sustainable Growth:**
- Revenue must exceed costs eventually
- Growth that burns cash must have payback
- Unit economics must improve with scale
- Build for 10X without breaking at 2X
